Bryant Road Development Information

Lexington, Kentucky

Project Summary

A development proposal has been submitted for property along Bryant Road near Timber Creek Drive in Lexington, Kentucky.

The plan proposes a mixed-use development including:

  • A hotel (approximately 95 rooms)
  • Multiple apartment buildings
  • Parking and internal streets
  • Stormwater detention areas

The documents below are the official planning materials submitted to Lexington Planning.

Project Location

The proposed development is located near Bryant Road and Timber Creek Drive.
